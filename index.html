FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What is Dental Appointment Engine?

DAE is an AI-powered patient acquisition and appointment booking platform for dental clinics focused on higher-value treatments.

Which treatments is DAE best suited for?

DAE is strongest for implant, cosmetic, Invisalign, smile makeover, and other high-value treatment campaigns where qualification and follow-up matter.

Does DAE replace our front desk or treatment coordinator?

No. DAE supports your team by qualifying patients, automating follow-up, organizing the pipeline, and surfacing the right next action.

How does DAE reduce lead leakage?

Every enquiry is captured, routed into a CRM stage, followed up automatically, and tracked until it is booked, disqualified, or needs human attention.
